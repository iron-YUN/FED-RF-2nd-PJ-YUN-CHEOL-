    IN COLLABORATION WITH NATHAN YONG

    LAST UPDATED: 26 MAR 2024

    The Macallan House Singapore encapsulates the spirit of The Macallan Estate through the lens of 'Nature Culture,' weaving a rich tapestry of multi-sensory experiences throughout its expansive 3,000-square-foot retail haven.

    The areas within The Macallan House Singapore have been thoughtfully crafted to lead visitors on an immersive exploration of the natural world. The cultural essence of the creative expression is exemplified by our collaboration with Nathan Yong, a Spatial, Furniture, and Object Designer.

    Nathan’s creations draw deep inspiration from The Macallan Estate, forging an exquisite union of artistic expression and the essence of our heritage.
